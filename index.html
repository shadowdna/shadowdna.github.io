📍Shadow Mechanism

The `Shadow` mechanism ensures that your assets are only under your control. Each Shadow account is a 32-byte hash (Shadow ID) generated from your wallet address and an Auth phrase, making it impossible for anyone to guess the real wallet address unless they know both(Auth & wallet-address). Each Shadow ID is cryptographically mapped to a wallet-address.
